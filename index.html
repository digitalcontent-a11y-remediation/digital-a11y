The 2027 deadline is real—and sooner than you think

The U.S. Department of Health and Human Services has extended Section 504 digital accessibility compliance deadlines to May 11, 2027. The ADA Title II compliance timeline extends to April 2027. UC institutions are aligning their IT Accessibility Policy (ITAP) with these federal deadlines, and WCAG 2.1 Level AA remains the governing technical standard.

Universities that delay face significant risks:

  • Regulatory enforcement actions and legal exposure
  • Mounting remediation costs as timelines compress
  • Continued barriers for students with disabilities
  • Institutional reputation and accreditation impact

The time to act is now. With roughly 18 months until federal deadlines, you can audit content systematically, prioritize high-impact areas (like PDFs and course notebooks), and build sustainable remediation workflows—instead of facing a crisis-mode scramble in 2026.
